A place for knowledge

Eduality is a platform where content creators pick a domain (from a wide variety of categories like science, photography, sports) and share papers, videos, podcasts in order to get points/recognition from other users for their content.

Promote yourself and get the recognition that you deserve

In modern society, certain subjects have been heavily controlled by a little group of people who decide what should and should not be popular. Eduality allows people to promote themselves and get the recognition that they deserve, to the extent of even getting the chance of working with brands we have agreements with.

This is a place for everyone

In our platform at the same time that we compensate and value our most experts users we make sure that newcomers influence and have a similar potential so that our ranking system is not exploited by a small minority with a lot of power.

How is all of this possible?

The magnificent work of 6 UMA students. We divided the activities into smaller tasks, after that we tried to solve them by our own, then we met to try to achieve the best possible solution for each task using the ideas that we developed alone. Once we got a common solution we checked if it fulfills the constraints of our main problem.
